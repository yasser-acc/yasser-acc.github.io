  • Our Missions

    The supernova team Goal is to provide high-quality website templates that are tailored specifically for junior developers , graphic designers, and web marketers. The team strives to create templates that are visually appealing, user-friendly, and customizable, enabling users to create professional and engaging websites with ease. The team members work collaboratively, leveraging their expertise and creativity to produce templates that meet the needs and preferences of their target audience. Through their dedication to quality and innovation, the team aims to empower aspiring web professionals to bring their ideas to life and make a positive impact in the online world.

  • Our Github repository offers pre-designed templates for personal or professional portfolio websites, suitable for a diverse range of individuals. Each template is uniquely designed and functional, created using HTML, CSS, and Javascript and optimized for different screen sizes and devices. Features include contact forms, social media integration, and customizable layouts. The repository is regularly updated with new templates, and users can easily download and customize them to showcase their skills and experiences with a professional look and feel.
